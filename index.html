Air Conditioning

Providing superior residential and commercial air conditioning solutions for any space, Goulburn Refrigeration and Air Conditioning specialise in split system air conditioning installation offering a large range of brands and products to suit any space and budget. Our fully licensed technicians will provide you with the highest level of service, maintenance and repair for any style of ducted or split system air conditioning. We also provide a hygienic deep clean service to correctly remove dust, bacteria and mould spores from your system improving air quality which helps with allergies and asthmatic sufferings. During this process we install SAN-AIR which is made using 100% natural and organic Australian ingredients which is proven to reduce mould and bacteria that it comes into contact with.

Commercial Refrigeration

With many years of experience in the petroleum and fast food industry at Goulburn Refrigeration and Air Conditioning we understand the importance of commercial refrigeration for our clients. We offer fast, effective solutions for all commercial refrigeration breakdowns and we carry a broad range of commercial parts and refrigerant gas on hand to get your system back on line sooner, avoiding down time and product loss. We also offer a maintenance program which will highlight any issues before breakdown occurs and increase longevity of your refrigeration systems and reduce energy running costs.
